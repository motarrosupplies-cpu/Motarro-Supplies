## 🔗 Internal Linking Strategy

### From Blog Posts, Link To:
1. **Product Pages** (3-5 per post)
   - Link to relevant products using natural anchor text
   - Example: "Check out our [custom t-shirts](#)" in a corporate branding post

2. **Category Pages**
   - Link to product categories (e.g., /men, /women)
   - Example: "Browse our [men's collection](#)" in a styling guide

3. **Service Pages**
   - Link to /custom-printing, /contact
   - Example: "Learn more about our [custom printing services](#)"

4. **Other Blog Posts**
   - Link to 2-3 related articles
   - Helps distribute link equity

### Best Practices:
- ✅ Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- ✅ Make links contextually relevant
- ✅ Limit to 2-3 external links per post
- ✅ Use HTTPS for all internal links
