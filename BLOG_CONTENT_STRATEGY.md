## **Content Creation Guidelines**

### **Article Structure**
1. **Compelling Headline** (include target keyword)
2. **Introduction** (hook reader, state value proposition)
3. **Main Content** (organized with H2, H3 headings)
4. **Conclusion** (summarize, include call-to-action)
5. **Related Articles** (internal linking)

### **SEO Requirements**
- **Word Count**: 800-2000 words per article
- **Keyword Density**: 1-2% (natural placement)
- **Headings**: Use H2, H3 tags with keywords
- **Meta Description**: 150-160 characters
- **Internal Links**: 2-3 per article
- **Images**: Alt text with keywords

### **Content Quality Standards**
- **Original Research**: Include unique insights
- **Visual Elements**: Images, infographics, videos
- **Expert Sources**: Cite industry authorities
- **Actionable Tips**: Provide practical value
- **Local Focus**: Include South African context
