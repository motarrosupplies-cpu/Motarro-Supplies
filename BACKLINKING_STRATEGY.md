## **Backlink Quality Criteria**

### **High-Quality Backlinks**
- **Domain Authority**: 40+ (preferably 50+)
- **Relevance**: Industry-related or complementary business
- **Traffic**: 10,000+ monthly visitors
- **Location**: South African or international fashion/apparel sites
- **Context**: Natural placement within relevant content
- **Anchor Text**: Brand name or relevant keywords

### **Medium-Quality Backlinks**
- **Domain Authority**: 20-39
- **Relevance**: Business-related or local South African sites
- **Traffic**: 1,000-10,000 monthly visitors
- **Context**: Directory listings or business profiles

### **Low-Quality Backlinks (Avoid)**
- **Domain Authority**: Below 20
- **Relevance**: Unrelated industries
- **Traffic**: Very low or no traffic
- **Context**: Spammy directories or link farms
- **Anchor Text**: Over-optimized or irrelevant

### **3. Broken Link Building**

1. **Identify Target Sites**: High-authority industry websites
2. **Find Broken Links**: Use tools like Ahrefs, Majestic
3. **Create Replacement Content**: Develop relevant content

5. **Follow Up**: Ensure links are properly placed
